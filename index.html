MELBA ROY MOUTON

Melba Roy (1929-1990) was a brilliant American Mathematician and Programmer.Melba Roy Mouton worked at NASA'S Goddard Space Flight Center, coding computer programs to calculate the trajectoried and locations of various aircrafts.

The amazing accomplishments of Melba Roy:

  • She recieved and Exceptional Perfomance Award and NASA's Apollo Achievement Award.
  • Graduated with Master's Degree in Mathematics from Howard university.
  • Her contribution to Echo helped produce the orbital elments from which millions saw the satellite from earth as it passed overhead.
  • She was the head of a group of NASA mathematician called "computers".
  • Retired in 1973.Sadly passed away in 1990 of a brain tumor in Silver Spring, MD.
